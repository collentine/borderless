Be a social media hero!

Helping Pirate Party Norway reach out on social media is one of the most important things we can do for them from the distance of our laptops. Facebook gives posts a more viral reach if they get many likes/shares immediately when published. Therefore we'd like to create a group of people that will boost their facebook reach 2-3 times per week.

This will be done by creating a list of supporters and texting persons as soon as the post is published. It's then your job to get on Facebook and like/share that post as soon as possible. Boosting posts in this way will make that post reach more of their followers as well as friends-of-friends = more Norwegians will see their updates.
